Blood Bank At Your Doorstep

Fill the form and we'll be at your doorstep to collect the blood!
An initiative by Bharatbhushan Barate


Maharashtra faces an acute shortage of blood due to the 3-week lockdown in order to prevent the novel coronavirus from spreading.

At present, there is a ban on organising blood donation camps. While there is no blood requirement for Covid-19 patients, other patients including those who undergo dialysis need it. State health minister Rajesh Tope said the stateâ€™s stock of blood will last one seven to eight days and appealed people to come forward to donate at blood banks.




Here are some Frequently Asked Questions!


1. Why should I donate blood?

Umm, this is nice question though. But, we will simply say, If you don't then who? There is no other method to manufacture blood; the only way to collect blood is from the people who are willing to donate. This is the ultimate donation human being can do in their life to the society. (If you donate blood regularly then it will reduce the risk of heart attack, cancer, high cholesterol level!)


2. What I can do for this?

Answer is very simple. You just have to click on the donate button and fill up the form.


3. How can I donate the blood from home?

Currently, we are in touch with blood banks, Once you fill the form, we will contact the blood banks and they will revert to you as soon as possible and they will to your doorstep.


4. How it works?

When you fill the form and submit it, we store it into our secured database. We contact to the nearest blood bank from your location. Once they get your information, they will connect with you and fix the date and time. Once they come to your location, you just have to spair 15 minutes and donate the blood! Got it?
